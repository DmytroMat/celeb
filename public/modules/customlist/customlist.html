Budget Range
Remember: we list the minimum average fee that the celebrities and their managers might confirm for. We cannot approach talent if your budget is much lower than published; even if the booking is intended for a charity event, a benefit for a "great cause", and especially not for personal requests by fans. Separately, they may quote a higher fee than what we list. Normally, celebrities only discount for close friends, family, or personal causes, if at all. All international offers must have a higher budget, than a domestic (US) event, to be considered, due to the additional logistics and traveling costs.
